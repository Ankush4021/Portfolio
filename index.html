What I do

I'm a frontend developer who enjoys building clean and smooth user interfaces. I focus on making websites easy to use, visually balanced, and fast to load. I pay attention to small details like spacing, readability, and subtle interactions that improve the user experience without feeling distracting.

My workflow is simple and practical. I design with real-world use in mind, write clean and reusable code, and test on different devices to make sure everything works well. I feel most satisfied when the UI looks good, feels natural to use, and the code remains easy to understand and maintain.
